Sustenato Pedal

Piano-like sustain for guitars and other analog instruments.

Features

Piano-like Sustain

Capture and hold any sound, whether it be a note, chord or even a percussive technique.

Pedal Support

Toggle the pedal by clicking it in the interface, or assign a keybind using your keyboard or a footswitch.

Support the Project

In order to raise compatibility with more platforms and DAWs, the plugin needs to be signed and verified as safe on all platforms. Any donations given will be put towards making this possible.
