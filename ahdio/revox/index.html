Get ReVox

A Soundboard, Sampler and Voice Changer, free and open-source.

For Windows 10/11

Features

Soundboard and Sampler

Import your favorite audio clips, customize how they sound, and play them through your microphone.

Capture sounds on the fly, whether it be a sound you hear or make, and reuse it later.

Voice Effects

Get creative with your voice! Use built-in audio effects to change how you sound.

Audio Monitoring

Hear your voice and soundboard, and make sure you sound good.

Track your FX

Never forget what effects are on your voice. Keep track using a subtle HUD.

*Does not work with Exclusive Fullscreen applications.

Keyboard-Specific Keybinds

Got a spare keyboard? Use that for your hotkeys, and never accidentally trigger a sound/effect again.

Built for Streamers

Route your audio such that recording software doesn't record you twice.

Setup and Forget

ReVox is completely transparent - get it setup, launch it with Windows, and forget it's even there until you need it!
